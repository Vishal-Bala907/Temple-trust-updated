Objective and Scope

Objective:

This website is for Temple Trust Donation so it provides those facilities, which are used in Temple trust website. It also saves the time of members and makes works easy.

  • Member can donate from home and also provide ration. Yes, this is the website of a temple trust. You can donate ration by giving your home address on this website. Staff of the Trust will come to your house and take the ration.
  • Member can book online Darshan and Pooja. Member can also book a darshan slot. Member can also book morning pooja schedule.

  • Payments: After confirming the request, they will be prompted for the payment to the management for the organization of the facilities to arrange. They have the option to pay through the interface using a debit card.
